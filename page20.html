The Product Ecosystem Model

Build interconnected products that guide customers through a journey:

  1. Entry Point: Low-cost ($5-20) introductory product
  2. Core Offer: Primary solution ($50-200) with most value
  3. Expansion: Complementary products/services
  4. Premium Tier: High-end offering ($500+)
  5. Community: Ongoing membership/group

Example: Free guide → Course → Templates → Coaching → Mastermind
