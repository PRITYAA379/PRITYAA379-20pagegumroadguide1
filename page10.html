Advanced strategies to grow from solo creator to sustainable business.

Scaling systems separate hobbyists from professional creators.

The 4 Phases of Scaling

1

Productize

Turn your knowledge into sellable products

Product Suite

Develop 3-5 complementary products at different price points ($29, $97, $297+)

Evergreen Updates

Refresh products quarterly to maintain value and trigger Gumroad's "Recently Updated" visibility

Bundle Strategy

Combine products at a discount (3 for price of 2) to increase average order value

2

Automate

Systems that work while you sleep

The Automated Customer Journey

  1. Lead Magnet: Free resource → Email signup
  2. Welcome Sequence: 3 emails over 5 days → First product offer
  3. Post-Purchase: Thank you + complementary offer
  4. Nurture Sequence: Weekly value emails → Premium offer

Tools to Automate: ConvertKit (emails), Zapier (workflows), Gumroad Post-Purchase Offers

3

Delegate

Multiply your output through others

  • Virtual Assistant: $5-15/hr for admin tasks
  • Freelancers: Designers, editors, video producers
  • Automation First: Only delegate what can't be automated
  • Gumroad Affiliates: 10-30% commission for promoters
4

Premiumize

Serve your best customers

$2,000+ Coaching
$497 Mastermind
$197 Course
$29 Ebook

Premium Offer Examples:

  • 1:1 consulting sessions ($500-2000)
  • Group coaching program ($997/year)
  • Mastermind community ($97/month)
  • Done-for-you services

Scaling Readiness Checklist:

  • Do you have at least 3 products generating consistent sales?
  • Have you automated your email sequences?
  • Are you spending more than 50% of time on $10/hr tasks?
  • Do you have customers who would pay 5-10x your current prices?
  • Is your Gumroad audience growing organically?
