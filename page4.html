Product Creation: From Idea to Finished Product

Transform your validated idea into a high-value digital product with this streamlined process.

Day 1-2

Outline Your Product

  • Define core outcome for your customer
  • Break into modules/sections (3-5 main parts)
  • Create bullet-point outline of all content
Day 3-5

Create Core Content

Focus on minimum viable product (MVP) first:

  • Write the main content (or record videos)
  • Skip perfection - aim for "good enough"
  • Use the 80/20 Rule Focus on the 20% of content that delivers 80% of the value for prioritization
Day 6-7

Add Enhancements

  • Create worksheets/cheatsheets
  • Add relevant case studies/examples
  • Include actionable exercises
Day 8-10

Polish & Package

  • Design simple but professional cover
  • Format for readability (clear headings, spacing)
  • Add table of contents
  • Create sales page copy (we'll cover this later)

Essential Creation Tools

WRITING Google Docs, Notion, Scrivener

DESIGN Canva (for non-designers), Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud

VIDEO Loom, Riverside.fm, Descript

AUDIO Audacity, Descript, Adobe Audition

ORGANIZATION Trello, Notion, ClickUp

Product Creation Principles:

  • Done is better than perfect - Launch then improve
  • Focus on transformation - What will customers achieve?
  • Make it scannable - Use clear formatting
  • Include action steps - Help customers apply knowledge
  • Set clear boundaries - Don't overpromise
"Your first product should take 2 weeks to create, not 2 months. You'll learn more from launching than from polishing."
