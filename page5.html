Pricing Strategies That Maximize Revenue

Set prices that attract buyers while maximizing your profit. Here's what works on Gumroad.

Gumroad Pricing Tiers

Basic

$19-49

Essential offering

  • Core product only
  • PDF/Video files
  • Basic support
  • Good for impulse buys
Most Popular

Standard

$49-97

Best value

  • Core + bonuses
  • Worksheets/templates
  • Community access
  • Email support

Premium

$97-197+

Complete package

  • Everything in Standard
  • 1-on-1 consultation
  • Custom solutions
  • Priority support

Psychological Pricing Tactics

Tactic Example Effect
Charm Pricing $97 instead of $100 Perceived as significantly cheaper
Price Anchoring Show $197 crossed out → $97 Creates perception of value
Tiered Pricing Basic/Standard/Premium Makes middle option appealing
Pay What You Want Minimum $19, average $47 Good for building audience

Gumroad-Specific Pricing Tips:

  • Test different price points (Gumroad makes this easy)
  • Offer time-limited discounts to create urgency
  • Consider "Pay What You Want" for new products
  • Add payment plans for higher-priced items ($97+)
  • Bundle products once you have multiple offerings
"Price based on value delivered, not time invested. A $29 product that solves a $1,000 problem is a bargain."
