Authority Positioning Allows 3-5X Higher Pricing

The Authority Pyramid

1. Foundational Knowledge (Free Content)
2. Practical Application (Products)
3. Unique Frameworks (Signature Methods)
4. Industry Recognition (Media & Partnerships)
Core Strategy

The 3-Pillar Positioning Method

  1. Specialize: "I help [specific audience] achieve [specific outcome]"
  2. Differentiate: "Unlike [alternative], my approach focuses on [unique angle]"
  3. Credentialize: "With [X years/results] proving this method works"
Example Positioning Statement: "I help first-time SaaS founders land their first 100 paying customers without paid ads, using organic growth systems we've proven at 3 YC startups."
