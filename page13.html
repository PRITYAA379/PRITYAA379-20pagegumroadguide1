How it works: Customers start with your entry product, experience great results, then naturally progress to higher-ticket offers where you deliver exponentially more value.

5 Powerful Stacking Strategies

The Tripwire

A low-cost ($7-27) entry product that converts cold audiences into buyers. Example: "The 5-Page SEO Blueprint"

The Bundle

Combine 3+ products at a "3 for 2" discount. Increases average order value by 30-50%.

The Continuity

Monthly membership with recurring revenue. Example: "$97/month Template Club"

The Done-For-You

Premium service add-on. Example: "$500 Custom Implementation Audit"

The Certification

Higher-priced program with credential. Example: "$1,497 Certified Specialist Program"

Product Stacking Checklist:

  • Do you have products at 3+ price points?
  • Have you created natural upgrade paths?
  • Are you using Gumroad's bundle feature?
  • Do you offer both DIY and done-for-you options?
  • Have you tested post-purchase upsells?

Gumroad Stacking Hacks

  • Use the "Post-Purchase Offer" feature to automatically suggest relevant upsells
  • Create limited-time bundle discounts to create urgency
  • Link products in descriptions to guide customers to related offers
  • Offer payment plans for higher-ticket items ($500+)
