Gumroad Feature Deep Dive

Master these powerful (but often overlooked) platform features to boost your sales.

Post-Purchase Offers

Available to all

Automatically show complementary products after purchase, when customers are most likely to buy again.

Implementation Steps:

  1. Go to Product ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Post-Purchase Offer
  2. Select a relevant product to recommend
  3. Write compelling offer text (focus on benefits)
  4. Optionally add a discount code
Pro Tip: Offer a "done-for-you" upgrade to your DIY product. Example: "Loved the course? Get the $500 implementation audit."

Customer Segmentation

Available to all

Tag and filter customers to create targeted email campaigns.

Powerful Segments to Create:

  • Customers who bought Product A but not Product B
  • High-ticket buyers ($500+)
  • Repeat customers (2+ purchases)
  • Customers who haven't bought in 90 days

Pay What You Want

Available to all

Let customers choose their price while setting a minimum amount.

Best uses:

  • New product launches (build initial audience)
  • Older products (breathe new life into them)
  • Lead magnets (collect emails from free downloads)
