Email Marketing That Sells

Build relationships and drive sales with these proven email strategies for Gumroad creators.

Email Drives 3-5X More Sales Than Social Media

For Gumroad creators, email subscribers convert at 2-3% compared to 0.5-1% from social media.

The Perfect Product Launch Sequence

1

Pre-Launch: Tease the Solution

3-7 days before launch, build anticipation:

Subject: "The [Problem] Solution You've Been Waiting For"

"I've been working on something special to help you with [specific pain point]... Stay tuned for Thursday's reveal!"

2

Launch Day: Full Reveal

Focus on transformation with clear CTA:

Subject: "It's Here: [Product Name] Now Available!"

"Remember how frustrated you felt when [problem]? With [product], you'll [benefit 1], [benefit 2], and [benefit 3]."

3

Day 2: Social Proof

Show testimonials or early results:

Subject: "[X] people are already using [Product]"

"Here's what early users are saying: [Testimonial 1] [Testimonial 2] Don't miss out - join them now!"

4

Day 3: Urgency Trigger

Limited-time offer or bonus:

Subject: "Last Chance: [Bonus] Expires Tonight"

"When you get [product] by midnight, you'll also receive [valuable bonus] absolutely free. This offer disappears in [hours] hours!"

5

Day 5: Final Reminder

Last call with strong why-now:

Subject: "Closing Soon: Your [Problem] Solution"

"This is your final opportunity to [achieve outcome] without [common struggle]. The doors close in [time left]."

Gumroad Email Best Practices:

  • Always include a clear call-to-action button linking to your Gumroad
  • Use Gumroad's UTM parameters to track email performance
  • Segment your list (buyers vs. non-buyers)
  • Send valuable content between promotions
  • Test different subject lines (emojis can increase opens)
